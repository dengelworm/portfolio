About Me

Hello! I'm Jia LIU, an illustrator, animator, graphic designer, and translator. I enjoy creating work that ranges from bright and playful children's illustrations to darker, more adult-oriented designs, as well as commercial graphic projects.

My artistic journey is guided by curiosity and experimentation, blending traditional techniques with digital media. I love exploring stories through visuals and creating experiences that resonate with diverse audiences.

In addition to my creative work, I offer translation services to bridge cultural gaps and help ideas reach a wider audience.
